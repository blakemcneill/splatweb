Become a member

We offer various levels of membership to fit your needs

Full

$60 / mo

  • 24x7 Access
  • Invitation to all Splat Space meetings
  • Free or discounted workshops and classes (minus materials)
  • Free access to hold meetings at the space
  • Full voting rights at all meetings

Associate

$25 / mo

  • Limited access to the space
  • Same benefits as the Full membership

Student

$35 / mo

  • All the benefits and access as the Full membership at a discounted rate for students
Full and Student members receive a key fob for full access. There is a $15 refundable deposit.
